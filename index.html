VERBO TO BE (SER O ESTAR)

El verbo to be (ser o estar) puede ser el más importante de la lengua inglesa. Por la cual es indespensable comprenderlo y dominarlo en todos sus tiempos y sus estructuras para tener un manejo del inglés, y para ello a continuación, veremos sus estructuras y conjugaciones.

ESTRUCTURAS

Afirmativa: (sujeto + verbo + complemento)
Negativa: (sujeto + verbo + not + complemento)
Interrogativa: (verbo + sujeto + complemento + ?)

AFIRMATIVA

1. I am a teacher

2. You are students

3. We are in class

4. They are at home

5. He is a doctor

6. She is in the kitchen

7. It is an easy task

NEGATIVA

1. I am not a teacher

2. You are not students

3. We are not in class

4. They are not at home

5. He is not a doctor

6. She is not in the kitchen

7. It is not an easy task

INTERROGATIVA

1. Am I a teacher?

2. Are you students?

3. Are we in class?

4. Are they at home?

5. Is he a doctor?

6. Is she in the kitchen?

7. Is it an easy task?
